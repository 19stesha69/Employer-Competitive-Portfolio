About Me

The internet is a window to the world.


Ever since I took my first steps on to the world wide web, I've been amazed by what I've found - ideas, cultures, and experiences that would otherwise require many trips around the globe, as well as numerous lifetimes, to discover. From that moment I was hooked, a happy denizen of a connected world.


When I found myself at a crossroads in my life, I decided to step out - far out - of my comfort zone and learn how to make a presence on the web. It has been challenging, and at times frustrating, but ultimately rewarding. Once I realized that a computer ins't an evil being, its will set against me, but rather a machine, operating according to its own, albeit curious, brand of logic, I stopped worrying and learned to love web design and devopment.


Along with a webDev toolbox that includes knowledge of HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Node.js, PHP, MySQL, React.js, MongoDB, Git, Heroku, and Python, I bring with me years of experience in retail sales, purchasing, visual merchandising, trend forecasting, and stock management. I also have experience writing and editing documents for business communication and public readership.


I currently reside in Beaverton, Oregon and when not playing with code I enjoy sewing, knitting, reading about history, and creative writing.
